The Great Smokey Mountains National Park

Experienced Traveler

Having visited "the Smokies" almost every year for the past several decades, I've accumulated some tips to make your vacation extra special.
Whether you chose to "rough it" or "be pampered", this primordial forested area can cater to all you desires.

Where to stay

Choose where to rest at night after a splendid day enjoying the breathtaking views.

  • Camping is intense/in tents!⛺😉
  • Historic Park Vista Hotel
  • Creekside Condos

Where to eat

Campfire meals, family style, casual or fine dining...so many choices!

  • Dutch Oven recipes
  • Foil Packet Cooking
  • Log Cabin Pankcake House
  • No Way José Cantina
  • Mellow Mushroom🍄
  • Hard Rock Café
  • Plant

Outdoor Adventures

You might want to plan on a return trip to see it all!

  • Laurel Falls
  • Rainbow Falls
  • Mouse Creek Falls
  • Clingmans Dome
  • Bicycle Cades Cove🚴.
  • Horseback riding
  • River tubing

Wildlife

  • Black Bears
